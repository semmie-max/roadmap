How tixtee. Roadmap Works

Launch Your Event Ecosystem

In under 10 minutes, you can set up a fully optimized event page with tiered ticketing, seamless checkout, and built-in travel bundles. Connect your venue to local hotels and shuttle routes so attendees can book their entire experience in one click. You set the markup on every room and ride — turning logistics into an automated, passive revenue stream before your first ticket even sells.

Sell Fast, Unlock Faster

Every ticket sale feeds real-time data into your dashboard. The moment your sales hit specific momentum thresholds, the platform automatically unlocks a portion of your ticket revenue directly to your bank account. No waiting until the show is over. Just your own earnings, accessible early, so you can cover deposits, artists, and marketing while the hype is still building.

Connect to teams

Now you have the insights to make next year even bigger. Dive into deep analytics: see which hotels sold out, which shuttle routes were most used, and exactly where your attendees came from. Manage payouts, tax docs, and check-ins all from one dashboard. You stop guessing and start growing. Because every event becomes a blueprint for the next.
